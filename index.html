What is StudyPeak?

StudyPeak is a mobile studying app that is designed to help people of all ages to study efficiantly. With our simple and user friendly UI users will be able to become familliar with all the apps features in no time. With almost everyone in the world having a smartphone we have decided that this wouldbe a great app to make because almost everyone will the ablility to download our app.

Its a Mobile App?

YES! With StudyPeak being a mobile device users will be able to study where ever they are. If you have no internet connectionn dont worry there is an offline mode where you will be able to study the material you have downloaded.


StudyPeak's Features

We have decded to have a friends feature on the app. This woould allow people to send requests to other people. By doing this both people could see eachothers progression on the "mountain" and could have a friendly competition to see who get the highest grades. We believe that this would motiavate users to use our app to study more.
