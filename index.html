About

Designed to be different.

StudyPeak is not just your run-of-the-mill study app. Designed to help students of all ages make the most of the time they spend studying, it includes an industry first feature: simply tell the app your what subjects you're good at, what ones aren't your forte and your hobbies, and it will generate a customised timetable with the aim of bringing up your grade in the weaker subjects while maintaining your performance in your strong subjects. Plus, it even incorporates your hobbies in to the timetable, becuase everyone deserves a break!

Features galore.

Everything you'd expect from a study app. And then some.

Customised timetables in an instant.

Remember you were going to make a study timetable but just couldn't be bothered? We weren't either. Then we created StudyPeak. Creating your timetable is as easy as 1, 2, 3!

1. Tell StudyPeak what you're studying.

2. Not so good at maths? Let StudyPeak know so it can factor in extra time to practice that algebra!
Master at English? Awesome, StudyPeak will make sure to limit the amount of Shakespeare study you do and dediacte that time to your weakest subjects!

3. Don't forget your hobbies! Tell StudyPeak whay your hobbies are so it can factor them into the timetable too.

Progress tracking made easy.

Just did a test? Input your result to StudyPeak. If it's a good result, StudyPeak might reward you with extra breaks and reduce the time you need to study for that subject. If it wasn't so good, StudyPeak will intelligently recalculate your timetable so you can spend a little more time at that subject and get back on track!

Feeling confident? Compete with your friends.

Add your classmates and prepare to see who can study the hardest. Every time you study or input a good test grade, you become one step closer to reaching the peak of the mountain. The harder you work, the better your progress (and grades) will be!

Stuck? Get unstuck with Tutorial Videos.

Did your maths teacher's explanation of differential calculus leave you feeling even more confused than before? Not to worry, StudyPeak Ultimate users get exclusive access to our collection of high-quality, easy-to-follow tutorial videos on over 20 of the most popular subjects.
